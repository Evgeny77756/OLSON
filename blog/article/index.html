The Future of Visual Design

DATE

May 7, 2020

READ TIME

2 MIN




What makes a room pop?

Is it the throw pillows? The carpet? Furniture? Often it’s something much simpler: the wallpaper. It isn’t the most obvious thing in the room, but it makes all the difference. Carla Morano of Fine and Dandy Co. creates new patterns and is changing the way we consider design and customer involvement.
Join Chris Snyder in this week’s episode of Snyder’s Marketing Showdown as he interviews Carla Morano about the evolution of creative design and how leveraging social media can expand your client network. Tune in as they discuss how Carla and her friends used their life’s work as an inspiration to build the company, the marketing strategy of Fine and Dandy, and how focusing on your clients’ needs can help you fine-tune your business.
